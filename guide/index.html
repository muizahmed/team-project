Our park has been designed with the needs of disabled guests taken into consideration. A special 50% off admission ticket is available at the ticketing counter. Kindly present ID for verification purposes. Risk assessments are conducted regularly and policies are reviewed annually to ensure that the Resort remains as accessible as possible to disabled guests, including wheelchair users.

First Aid is located next to the Gran Prix. First Aid is operated by UPMC. Refrigeration for essential medicine is available on a limited basis. In an emergency, please contact any park Team Member.

ATM machine is available at The Beginning.

Please report any lost articles or return any found items to Guest Services Counter or any Park Ranger. The park is not responsible for theft, damage or loss of personal property

While there are many “hands-on” activities at the park, some attractions, such as MINILAND, are for viewing enjoyment only. It is the responsibility of each parent or guardian to supervise those under their care while in the park. Each attraction experience is uniquely different. Ride and attraction requirements are determined by the ride dynamics, restraint system used and manufacturer’s recommendations. Ride and attraction information and restrictions (such as height, health, size and age) are posted at each location. We appreciate your understanding of our need to provide a safe environment for guests.
